What

DAVID.COMPUTER is a digital garden where I share my work, interests, and upcoming events. Much like the now-defunct Jeremy Renner app (RIP), this is where you'll find exclusive content and updates first. The key difference? This space is designed to be more intentional, moving away from the often overwhelming nature of traditional social media platforms.

This site serves as a central hub for all my updates and content, from art exhibitions and coding projects to thoughts and experiments. By creating this dedicated space, I aim to foster more meaningful interactions and showcase my work in a way that feels authentic and purposeful.

Through this platform, I hope to build genuine connections with others who share similar interests in technology, art, and design, while maintaining a level of intimacy that's often lost in larger social networks.
