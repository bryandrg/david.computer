Who

Hi, I'm David! I'm a computer science graduate from New Mexico State University with a love for blending technology, creativity, and design. I enjoy creating both digital spaces—like functional interfaces and websites—and tangible works of art, such as drawings, prints, and photography. My goal is to build experiences that are thoughtful, engaging, and accessible.

I built this site as a digital garden—a quiet corner of the internet where I can share my ideas, interests, and work as they grow. Feel free to explore, and if you'd like to connect, you can reach me at contact@david.computer.
